# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Inlet: Mindset & Motion** handles information when you use the app.

## Information we may collect

We may collect:

- account-related information you provide, such as sign-in identifiers
- chat messages you send to the AI coach
- technical or device information needed to operate the service, secure the app, and support notifications if enabled
- purchase or transaction status needed to provide paid features, if applicable

## How we use information

We may use information to:

- provide AI coaching responses and core app features
- keep your account signed in and synced
- operate, secure, debug, and improve the service
- manage subscriptions, credits, reminders, and related account functions

## Chat records and AI processing

Your chat messages may be processed and stored on our servers to generate AI coach responses and operate the service. Because the service is AI-based, please do **not** enter sensitive personal information, health records, financial account details, passwords, government identification numbers, or other highly confidential content in chat.

## Sharing and tracking

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your chat records with third parties. We do not use your chat records or other app information to track you across third-party apps, websites, or services.

## Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our terms.

## Your choices

You can stop using the app at any time. Depending on the features available in the current version, you may also manage notifications and certain local app settings on your device.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The version shown in the app is the current version.
